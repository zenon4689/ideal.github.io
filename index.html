Welcome to IDEA.L

INTELLIGENT DATA-CENTRIC EMERGING ARCHITECTURE LAB

The IDEA lab is investigating various hardware and software challenges related to System-on-Chip
architecture and design technology implemented as semiconductor chips.

We are recruiting self-motivated students (graduates and undergraduate interns)
who want to explore and uncover new knowledge to change the world.

AI Acceleration

We design new AI acceleration techniques for System-on-Chip(SoC) technologies, including deep-learning specific instruction-set processors, energy-efficient computer arithmetic, and high-performance computing system.

SoC On-Chip Network

Network-on-Chip technology is an efficient data transmission hardware architecture system. We design faster System-on-Chip(SoC) for efficient algorithms that take workload characteristics into account.

Near Data Processing

NDP has great opportunities in terms of accelerating computation and reducing power consumption. We solve the challenge of NDP by utilizing system-level simulation.
